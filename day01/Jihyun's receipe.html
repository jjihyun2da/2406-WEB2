Welcome to my recipe page! Here you'll find a step-by-step guide to making this delicious dish.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Kimchi
  • Bacon
  • Green Onion
  • Onion
  • Egg
  • Sesame Oil
  • Sesame
  • Oil
  • Pepper Powder

Preparation Steps

  • Step 1 Cut the Napa cabbage into 1 cm square pieces, and chop the onion coarsely.
    Slice the green onions, and cut the bacon into bite-sized pieces.
    Mix the seasoning ingredients in a bowl.

  • Step 2 Heat a pan with a bit of oil and cook the bacon until itâ€™s browned.
    Then add the onion and kimchi, and stir-fry over medium heat for about 2 minutes.
    Next, add the seasoning and mix everything together.

  • Step 3 Add the rice and stir-fry, mixing it thoroughly with a spatula as if dividing the rice.
    Finally, add the sesame oil.

  • Step 4 Serve the kimchi fried rice in a bowl, and sprinkle with sesame seeds and sliced green onions.
    Adding a fried egg on the side enhances the flavor.
